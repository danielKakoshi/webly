Our Story

A Passion for
Timeless Craft

Aurum was born from a deep love for honest cooking — where the integrity of an ingredient speaks louder than any technique. Our head chef, trained under Michelin-starred kitchens in Lyon and Paris, returned home with a singular vision: to honour local harvests through a fine-dining lens.

"We do not cook food. We compose moments — tableaux of texture, scent, and memory that linger long after the last bite."

— Chef Etienne Moreau, Founder
